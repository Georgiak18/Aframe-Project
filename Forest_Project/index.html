The Forest

To win do the following:

    Collect 10 fruit

    Avoid the Animals

    Utilize safe zone

    To move around the world, use the arrows keys and to collect the fruit use the mouse.

    The general purpose of my virtual reality experience is to allow the user to explore the forest and interact with the trees; picking apples from them and everytime a fruit is collected, a counter will show up on the screen.

    The user will also be chased by animals such as wolves and bears once they get too close.

    When you win and you want to play again, refresh your screen.
